Response Email Monitoring

Configure Gmail and/or Outlook accounts below. The daemon checks them every hour for replies from companies you've applied to, automatically detects declines and interview invitations, updates your application status, and saves each email to email_responses/ in the app directory. Both accounts are checked simultaneously.

Gmail Recommended imap.gmail.com · Port 993 · SSL
Requires a Google App Password — not your regular Gmail password. Generate one here (Google Account → Security → 2-Step Verification → App Passwords → Select app: Mail).
Outlook / Hotmail / Live outlook.office365.com · Port 993 · SSL
Microsoft does not allow your regular password for IMAP — you must use an App Password.
  1. Enable two-step verification on your Microsoft account if it isn't already: account.microsoft.com/security
  2. Go to Advanced security options → App passwords and create a new one named Mail.
  3. Enable IMAP access: sign in to outlook.live.com → Settings (gear) → View all Outlook settings → Mail → Sync email → enable IMAP.
  4. Paste the generated app password in the field above (not your account password).

Calendar Integration

Subscribe to your personal Job Tracker calendar feed — no sign-in, no OAuth. All your interviews and follow-up reminders appear in a dedicated calendar that stays in sync automatically. Events use the timezone set in your Scan Schedule settings below.

Sync delay: Google Calendar only polls subscribed feeds every 8–24 hours. After saving changes in the app, right-click the Job Tracker calendar in the left sidebar and choose Refresh to force an immediate update. Outlook syncs similarly — open the calendar settings and click Update.

Your Calendar Feed URL
Keep this URL private — it grants read access to your calendar events. Regenerate it if you ever share it accidentally.
Subscribe in Google Calendar
  1. Open Google Calendar in your browser
  2. In the left sidebar click + next to Other calendars
  3. Choose From URL
  4. Paste the feed URL above and click Add calendar
  5. A new Job Tracker calendar appears with your events
  6. To force an update after saving changes: right-click Job Tracker in the sidebar → Refresh
Subscribe in Outlook
  1. Open Outlook (web or desktop)
  2. Go to Calendar → Add calendar
  3. Choose Subscribe from web
  4. Paste the feed URL above, give it a name (e.g. Job Tracker), and click Import

Benefit Period & Claim Week
Claim weeks are generated forward from your start date in 7-day increments. The end date defaults to exactly one year after the start date.
Claim Deadline Alarms — notify when required applications not yet met
An in-app notification is created each day an alarm is triggered until the weekly requirement is met. Alarms escalate in severity: Reminder (grey) → Warning (yellow) → Alert (red).
Database Management
Reset Database

Permanently deletes all jobs, applications, scan logs, and notifications from the database and resets all ID counters back to 1. This cannot be undone.

Warning: All tracked jobs and application history will be lost forever.
Repair & Recalculate

Re-scores every job against your current skill settings, updates job categories and duration labels, and removes any orphaned notification records. Safe to run at any time.

Use this after updating your required or preferred skills to refresh all match scores.
Delete All Jobs?

This will permanently delete:

  • All applications and their history
  • All scan logs
  • All notifications

This action cannot be undone.
