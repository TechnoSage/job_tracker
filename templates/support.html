Reporting a Bug

A good bug report includes:

  • Exact steps to reproduce the issue
  • What you expected vs what happened
  • Any error messages or screenshots
  • Browser, OS, and app version
Common Issues

Go to Settings → Scheduler and confirm it is enabled. Check that scheduler_daemon.pid exists. Restart the daemon from the Build Dashboard.

No skills are configured. Go to Settings → Skills, add keywords, then re-run a scan.

Reduce active sources. A specific source may be blocking automated requests — disable it if it always times out.

Verify SMTP credentials in Settings → Notifications and use Test Email to confirm the connection.

Stop the app, delete instance/jobs.db, then restart. Settings and skills are unaffected.
