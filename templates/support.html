Common Issues

Go to Settings → Scheduler and confirm it is enabled. Check that scheduler_daemon.pid exists in the app directory. Restart the daemon from the Build Dashboard if needed.

No skills have been configured. Go to Settings → Skills and add the keywords you want to match against. Re-run a scan to update scores.

Reduce the number of active sources in Sources. Check your internet connection. If a specific source always times out, disable it — the site may be blocking automated requests.

Stop the app, delete instance/jobs.db, then restart. All jobs and scan history will be cleared. Settings and skills are stored separately and are unaffected.

Go to Settings → Notifications and verify your SMTP credentials. Use Test Email to confirm the connection works. Check that your email provider allows app passwords or SMTP access.

Use Analytics → Export to download a CSV of your jobs. For a full database backup, copy instance/jobs.db to a safe location.
Reporting a Bug

The more detail you provide, the faster we can fix it. A good bug report includes:

  • The exact steps to reproduce the issue
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened (error message / screenshot)
  • Your browser, OS, and app version
  • Whether the issue is consistent or intermittent
